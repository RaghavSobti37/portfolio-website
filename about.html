03 : About me

Collaborative Creation

Collaboration lies at the heart of creativity, bringing together individuals with shared goals to produce richer and more meaningful stories. With experience spanning various photography and videography projects, from social media content to indie outdoor shoots, the emphasis has always been on creating cohesive and impactful visual narratives.

Passion for Visual Storytelling

A deep passion for photography and cinematography drives the pursuit of capturing moments that resonate emotionally. Inspired by everyday life, nature, and human connections, the work reflects a commitment to telling compelling stories through the lens, whether on a set in Kashmir or crafting dynamic visuals for social media platforms.

Personal Style and Vision

The focus remains on delivering visual stories that connect with audiences and foster lasting impressions. Always open to new collaborations, the work brings fresh perspectives to any project.
